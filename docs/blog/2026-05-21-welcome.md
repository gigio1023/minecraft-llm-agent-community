Welcome to the official blog of **Dream of One**, an open-source project dedicated to exploring social simulation and multi-agent systems within Minecraft.

<!--truncate-->

## Our Vision

We believe that Minecraft is more than just a game—it's a powerful laboratory for studying Artificial Intelligence. Our goal is to create NPCs that don't just follow scripts, but interact with each other in meaningful ways, driven by their own needs, roles, and social obligations.

## What's Next?

In the coming weeks, we'll be sharing:
- **Technical Deep Dives**: How we use [Mineflayer](https://github.com/PrismarineJS/mineflayer) and LLMs to power our NPCs.
- **Simulation Reports**: Observations from our multi-bot experiments.
- **Architecture Updates**: Our progress on building a headless, deterministic runtime for AI research.

If you're interested in AI, social simulation, or just want to see what happens when you give Minecraft bots a "brain," follow along!

Check out our [Documentation](/docs/intro) to learn more about the project.
