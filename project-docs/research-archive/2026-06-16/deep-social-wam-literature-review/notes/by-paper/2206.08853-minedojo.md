# MineDojo — Building Open-Ended Embodied Agents with Internet-Scale Knowledge

- **authors**: Fan, Wang, Jiang, Mandlekar, Yang, Zhu, Tang, Huang, Zhu, Anandkumar (NVIDIA et al.)
- **year**: 2022 — **venue**: NeurIPS 2022 (Outstanding Datasets & Benchmarks paper)
- **arxiv_id**: 2206.08853
- **urls**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2206.08853 ; code https://github.com/MineDojo/MineDojo ; site https://minedojo.org

## Primary-source facts

- **Research question**: provide an open-ended agent platform plus an
  internet-scale knowledge base to support generalist Minecraft agents.
- **Simulator**: a Gym-style environment built on MineRL/Malmo exposing a unified
  observation/action interface and **thousands of open-ended + programmatic
  tasks** (the MineDojo task suite, with natural-language goals and a programmatic
  success detector for many tasks).
- **Knowledge base** (the lasting asset): **~730k YouTube videos + transcripts
  (~2.2B words)**, **~7k Wiki pages**, and **~340k Reddit posts** — the corpus
  used to train **MineCLIP** (a video-text contrastive model later reused by
  STEVE-1 and many others as a reward/eval signal).
- **Observation space**: RGB + compass/GPS-like info, voxel/lidar options, plus
  inventory and life stats depending on wrapper. **Action space**: multi-discrete
  over movement, camera, and functional actions (craft/equip/etc.) — a higher-
  level interface than VPT's raw keyboard+mouse.
- **Evaluation**: programmatic success for crafting/harvesting tasks; for creative
  tasks, MineCLIP-based scoring as an automatic proxy.

## Interpretation (inference)

- MineDojo is a **benchmark + simulator + knowledge corpus**, not an agent and not
  a WAM. Its enduring contribution to the whole field is **MineCLIP** and the
  notion that internet knowledge can be distilled into a reward/eval signal.
- For this repo, MineDojo matters as (a) the origin of MineCLIP (which appears as
  an evaluator throughout the VLA literature — and which this repo should keep as
  *audit*, not truth), and (b) a precedent for "thousands of tasks with
  programmatic detectors" — though MineDojo's tasks are largely command/structure
  fixtured, like MineStudio's.

## Mechanically useful vs research contribution

- **Mechanically useful**: the *idea* of pairing each task with a programmatic
  success detector; the MineCLIP eval signal as a secondary audit dimension; the
  multi-discrete functional action space as a mid-level reference point.
- **What would be overclaim**: using MineCLIP scores as runtime truth; treating
  MineDojo's task breadth as social breadth (it is competence/task breadth).

## WAM layer(s) informed

Physical-WAM (simulator + task detectors) and the **data ecosystem** (MineCLIP).
Reproducible: simulator + knowledge base public; full YouTube corpus is large and
partially access-gated.
