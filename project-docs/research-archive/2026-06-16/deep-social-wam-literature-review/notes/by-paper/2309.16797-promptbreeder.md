## Primary-source facts (LaTeX-verified)

- **Method**: Promptbreeder (PB) is an evolutionary algorithm where the mutation operator is itself an LLM. It mutates a population of TASK-PROMPTS, evaluates their fitness on a training set, and repeats over generations. A unit of evolution = (typically two task-prompts) + a MUTATION-PROMPT (the instruction used to mutate the task-prompt), in 1:1 correspondence.
- **Self-referential mechanism (the contribution)**: PB improves not only task-prompts but also the mutation-prompts that improve them. A mutated task-prompt P' = LLM(M + P) where M is a mutation-prompt; a mutated mutation-prompt M' = LLM(H + M) where H is a hyper-mutation prompt. So PB "is not just improving task-prompts, but it is also improving the mutation-prompts that improve these task-prompts." Five classes of mutation operators are used. Selection is a binary tournament genetic algorithm (sample two units, mutate the fitter, overwrite the loser).
- **Initialization**: from a seed set of mutation-prompts, "thinking-styles" (general cognitive heuristics, e.g. "Let's think step by step"), and a domain problem description. Fitness of a task-prompt is evaluated on a random batch of 100 Q&A pairs from the training set.
- **Substrate argument**: cites Schmidhuber ("the program of a neural network is its weight matrix"; self-referential weight matrices; Godel Machine). PB makes the substrate of self-referential self-improvement NATURAL LANGUAGE (prompts as the LLM's "program"), avoiding parameter updates entirely.
- **Results (as stated)**: PB outperforms Chain-of-Thought and Plan-and-Solve prompting on arithmetic and commonsense reasoning benchmarks, and evolves intricate prompts for hate-speech classification.
- **Landmark fact vs OPRO (a striking quote)**: "At the time of its release, OPRO achieved a score of 80.2% via the optimized zero-shot prompt 'Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step' on GSM8K. Promptbreeder surpasses this with 83.9% in the zero-shot setting with the unintuitively simple prompt 'SOLUTION\"' --- further evidence for the sensitivity of LLMs to prompts and the importance on finding effective prompts automatically."
- **Stated diagnosis of APE (motivation)**: APE (Automatic Prompt Engineer) "found diminishing returns to further selection rounds as the quality seems to stabilize after three rounds, and consequently abandoned the use of an iterative APE." PB claims to fix this via a diversity-maintaining evolutionary algorithm + self-referential mutation.

## Interpretation (flagged as inference)

- The "SOLUTION\"" result is the single most quotable fact in this lane: an optimized prompt that beats a carefully phrased human prompt (and beats OPRO's "take a deep breath") is a near-meaningless string. It proves the selection criterion CANNOT be human plausibility or an LLM judge of prompt quality; only the fitness score on held-out data legitimizes such a prompt.
- PB is also the lane's clearest example of "self-referential" improvement that is actually demonstrated and bounded (it evolves the mutation-prompts), as opposed to speculative recursive self-improvement. It connects this lane to the recursive-self-improvement-theory lane (H1/29) and the Schmidhuber Godel-Machine lineage, but the demonstrated mechanism is a genetic algorithm over text, not unbounded recursion.
- Fitness on a random batch of 100 training Q&A is a noisy signal; PB's diversity maintenance and tournament selection are partly an answer to the overfitting/noise problem OPRO documents.

## Mechanically useful vs research contribution

- **Mechanically useful**: (1) the LLM-as-mutation-operator + genetic-algorithm pattern for evolving the Actor Turn / advisory-WAM prompt; (2) the self-referential layer (evolve the prompt that mutates the prompt) as an optional second loop; (3) diversity maintenance to avoid APE-style diminishing returns; (4) thinking-styles as a seed library of cognitive heuristics.
- **What would be overclaim**: PB optimizes prompt TEXT for QA/reasoning/classification with a training-set fitness. It is not an embodied or social method, and "self-referential self-improvement" here is bounded prompt evolution, not open-ended recursive self-improvement. Do not import the grand framing; import the mechanism.
- **Honest bound for thesis**: the "SOLUTION\"" prompt is the proof that the verifier, not human judgment, must score candidates. For this repo, any PB-evolved actor prompt must be scored by the runtime verifier on held-out scenarios, and adopted only advisory + verifier-gated.
