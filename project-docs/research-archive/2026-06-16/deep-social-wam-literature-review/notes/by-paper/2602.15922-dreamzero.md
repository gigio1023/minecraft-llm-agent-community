# DreamZero - World Action Models are Zero-shot Policies

- **title**: World Action Models are Zero-shot Policies (DreamZero)
- **authors**: Seonghyeon Ye, Yunhao Ge, ... Yuke Zhu, Linxi "Jim" Fan, Joel Jang (NVIDIA)
- **year**: 2026
- **venue/source**: NVIDIA tech report / arXiv
- **arxiv_id**: 2602.15922
- **urls**: project https://dreamzero0.github.io ; code https://github.com/dreamzero0/dreamzero ; LaTeX at `papers/latex/2602.15922/`
- **source availability**: latex (full)

## Primary-source facts

- **Problem/output**: jointly predicts video `o_{l:l+H}` and actions `a_{l:l+H}` conditioned on language `c`, proprioceptive state `q_l`, visual history `o_{0:l}`. Decomposition (cascaded form realized in one model): `π(o,a|...) = π(o|o_{0:l},c,q), π(a|o_{0:l+H},q)` - video prediction × inverse-dynamics model (IDM).
- **Architecture**: 14B autoregressive Diffusion Transformer, initialized from a **pretrained image-to-video diffusion backbone (Wan2.1-14B)**. VAE for visual context, text encoder, state encoder; separate video and action decoders; flow matching; trained with teacher-forcing chunk-wise video denoising. During inference, predicted frames are replaced with ground-truth observations in the KV cache to stop error accumulation.
- **Action/observation**: continuous robot action chunks; RGB observation (multi-view concatenated into one frame); proprioception.
- **Dataset**: ~500 hours real-world robot data (heterogeneous, non-repetitive); also video-only human/other-robot data for transfer.
- **Training objective**: joint flow-matching denoising of video + action latents.
- **Eval**: RoboArena (real-world), PolaRiS and Genie Sim 3.0 (sim, 100 tasks). Metric = task progress / success rate.
- **Headline results**: >2x improvement in zero-shot generalization to **unseen verbs/motions** vs SOTA VLAs; cross-embodiment transfer from 12-20 min of video-only data (>42% relative gain); few-shot embodiment adaptation with 30 min play data. "policy performance is fundamentally tied to video generation quality."
- **Latency**: 38x inference speedup via algorithmic (decoupled video/action denoising = DreamZero-Flash), system (parallelism, caching), and low-level (quantization, CUDA) optimizations -> **~7Hz** closed-loop control. The survey notes this is still far below the 50Hz standard of non-generative VLAs.
- Authors' own modality note: "We use World Action Models (WAMs) rather than Video Action Models (VAMs) ... video is just one possible world modeling objective - future WAMs may align actions with other predictive modalities such as tactile sensing, force feedback, or learned latent representations." Contrast with latent WMs (Dreamer, V-JEPA2) which "learn dynamics from scratch in compact latent spaces" - DreamZero instead reuses pretrained video priors.
- **Weights**: open-sourced (model weights + inference code).

## Interpretation

- This is the canonical "WAM-as-zero-shot-policy" claim and the clearest cost/latency datapoint: a 14B video-diffusion WAM hits 7Hz only after a 38x engineering push. For a Minecraft runtime where the LLM Actor Turn already runs at multi-second cadence, a pixel-video WAM is both unnecessary (no pixel control loop) and far too heavy.
- The IDM decomposition `p(a|o,o')` is the mechanically interesting part: action is read *out of* a predicted future. The project's analogue would predict a structured future state (social-material delta) and read evidence/consequence out of it - but the project's hard rule is the LLM/runtime selects the action, so this stays advisory.

## Mechanically useful vs research contribution

- **Useful**: the cascaded `predict-future-then-derive-action` pattern as a *conceptual* template for an advisory predictor; the explicit statement that video is one of several predictive modalities (license to use latent/structured `o'`); the latency evidence to justify NOT using pixel WAM.
- **Avoid/overclaim**: adopting DreamZero's pixel-video backbone or its zero-shot-generalization numbers as relevant to a structured social WAM. Those results are robot manipulation; they do not transfer to social-material state.

## WAM layer(s) informed

Primarily definitional / Physical. Evidence for the cost/latency argument (why pixel WAM is not the path).
