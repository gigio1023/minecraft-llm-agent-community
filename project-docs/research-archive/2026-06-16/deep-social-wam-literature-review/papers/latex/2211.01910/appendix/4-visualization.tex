\section{Additional Visualizations}

\paragraph{Visualization Hyperparameters}As we tuned the hyperparameters of \algname including the number of proposals generated per demonstration and the number of demonstrations per random seed, we discovered better ones for instruction induction. We re-evaluated \algname on 5 tasks, giving human-level performance on all 24 of 24 instruction induction tasks. The additional visualizations below were based on a previous iteration of \algname which only reached human level on 19 of 24 tasks. The mean test accuracy differences for those 5 tasks are summarized in Table \ref{tab:ape-old-vs-new}.


\begin{table}[th]
  \caption{\algname hyperparameter tuning improvements on instruction induction.}
  \label{tab:ape-old-vs-new}
  \small
  \centering
    \begin{tabular}{cccc}
        \toprule
        Task Name & \algname (Old) Accuracy, Mean  & \algname (New) Accuracy, Mean & \algname (New) - Human \\
        \midrule
        Second Letter & 0.596 & 0.8 & 0.034 \\
        Pluralization & 0.984 & 0.996 & -0.004\\
        Passivization & 0.622 & 1 & 0.001 \\
        Sentence Similarity & 0.186 & 0.256 & -0.01 \\
        Membership & 0.126 & 0.612 & -0.001 \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
\end{table}
