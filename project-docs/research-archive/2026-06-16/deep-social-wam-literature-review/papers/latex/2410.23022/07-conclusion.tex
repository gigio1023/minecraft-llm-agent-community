\section{Conclusion}
We have introduced \oni, a distributed online intrinsic reward and agent learning system. We showed that we are able to match the state of the art across a range of challenging sparse reward tasks from the NetHack Learning Environment, while removing the need for a large pre-collected dataset or auxiliary dense reward function required by previous work. We explored three different instantiations of our system of varying levels of complexity and generality, and study their tradeoffs. Our work paves the way for intrinsic reward methods which can learn purely from agent experience, are not constrained by external dataset size or quality, and can leverage high-performance RL training. \looseness=-1
