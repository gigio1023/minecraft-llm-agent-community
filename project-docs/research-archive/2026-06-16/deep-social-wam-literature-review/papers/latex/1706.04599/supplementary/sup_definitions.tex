\label{sup:definitions}

We can connect the ECE metric with our exact miscalibration definition, which is restated here:
%
\begin{align*}
  \E_{\ph} \left[ \left| \P\left(\yh = Y \sep \ph = p\right) - p \right| \right]
  \label{eqn:miscalibration}
\end{align*}
%
Let $F_{\ph}(p)$ be the cumulative distribution function of $\ph$ so that $F_{\ph}(b) - F_{\ph}(a) = \P(\ph \in [a,b])$. Using the Riemann-Stieltjes integral we have
%
\begin{flalign*}
&\E_{\ph} \left[ \left| \P\left(\yh = Y \sep \ph = p\right) - p \right| \right] \\
&= \int_0^1 \left| \P\left(\yh = Y \sep \ph=p\right) - p \right| dF_{\ph}(p) \\
&\approx \sum_{m=1}^{M} \left| \P(\yh=Y|\ph = p_m) - p_m \right| \P(\ph \in I_m)
%&\approx \sum_{m=1}^{M} \left| \P(\yh=Y|\ph \in I_m) - \hat{p}(B_m) \right| \P(\ph \in I_m) \\
%&= \sum_{m=1}^M \left| \text{acc}(B_m)  - \hat{p}(B_m) \right| \P(\ph \in I_m).
\end{flalign*}
%
where $I_m$ represents the interval of bin $B_m$. $\left| \P(\yh=Y|\ph = p_m) - p_m \right|$ is closely approximated by $\left| \text{acc}(B_m)  - \hat{p}(B_m) \right|$ for $n$ large. Hence ECE using $M$ bins converges to the $M$-term Riemann-Stieltjes sum of $\E_{\ph} \left[ \left| \P\left(\yh = Y \sep \ph = p\right) - p \right| \right]$.
