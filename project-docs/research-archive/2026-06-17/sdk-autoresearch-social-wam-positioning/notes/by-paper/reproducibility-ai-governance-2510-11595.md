## Framing (VERIFIED at abstract/secondary level)

- Reproducibility is treated as a frontier issue for AI governance: claims that
  cannot be independently re-derived are weak inputs to policy and to scientific
  consensus. Publication bias and the absence of released artifacts are named as
  contributors to an unreliable evidence base.
