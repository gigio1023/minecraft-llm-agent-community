Maslow categorized the motivations of individual behavior into five levels \cite{1943A}. Clayton Alderfer refined and simplified this into the ERG theory, categorizing needs into three types: Existence, Relatedness, and Growth \cite{ALDERFER1969142}. We apply the ERG theory to categorize individual goals in social interactions, as shown in Table~\ref{tab:social_goal}
