\Cref{tab:appendix:conf-interval-main-results} shows results from \Cref{tab:results} with Wilson confidence interval \cite{brown2001_wilson_interval} (at $\alpha$= 99\% confidence interval) and statistical significance. Gains that are statistical significance based on these confidence intervals are marked with an asterisk. We find that nearly all of \gptf gains are statistically significant, \chatgpt gains are significant for 4 out of 7 datasets, and \gptt gains are significant for 3 out of 7 datasets.
