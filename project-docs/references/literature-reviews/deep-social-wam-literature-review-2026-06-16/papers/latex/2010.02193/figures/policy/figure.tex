The trajectories start from posterior states computed during model training and predict forward by sampling actions from the actor network. The critic network predicts the expected sum of future rewards for each state. The critic uses temporal difference learning on the imagined rewards. The actor is trained to maximize the critic prediction, via reinforce gradients, straight-through gradients of the world model, or a combination of them.}
