## Primary-source facts (abstract-verified)

- It is a POSITION paper (no system, no benchmark, not Minecraft).
- Claim: world models are advancing at predicting BOTH physical dynamics AND aspects of social
  behavior, "yet predominantly in separate silos".
- Thesis: "the systematic, bidirectional unification of physical and social predictive
  capabilities is the next crucial frontier for world model development."
